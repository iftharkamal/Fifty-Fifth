• ABOUT US
    •

    Fifty-Fifth is a full service creative studio in the heart of London. We work with agencies and directly with brands to deliver films people remember.

    We are owned and run by craftspeople;
    directors, designers, editors, producers. Every ounce of that in-house creative know-how is poured into all the work we make, be it a 30 second commercial or a 30 minute documentary.
